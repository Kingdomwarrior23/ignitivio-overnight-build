## MICRO-BUSINESS RESEARCH: FOOD TRUCKS, MARKET VENDORS, SOLOPRENEURS

### Food Trucks — Lubbock TX
- **Revenue:** $20K-$42K/mo ($250K-$500K/yr national avg)
- **Profit margin:** 3-5% (THIN — $800-$2,100/mo profit)
- **Marketing budget:** $500-$5,000/mo (3-6% of sales) BUT most actually spend $200-$500
- **What they need:** Daily location posts, menu highlights, event/promo promotion, hungry-customer search visibility
- **Why $797/mo fails:** Eats 38-100% of their profit. Unaffordable.
- **Why $297/mo works:** = 1.4% of $20K revenue. 5 extra customers/day at $15 = $2,250/mo. ROI = 7.6x
- **Key insight:** Food trucks live and die by LOCATION + TIMING. Social media that posts "We're at X location today until 2pm" with food photos is their #1 growth lever.

### Market Vendors (Farmers Markets, Pop-ups, Craft Fairs)
- **Revenue:** $2K-$8K/mo (depends on market frequency)
- **Marketing budget:** $100-$300/mo max
- **What they need:** Market day promotion, new product reveals, seasonal specials, between-market engagement
- **Why $297/mo works:** Builds a following that buys between markets. Turns one-time browsers into repeat customers.
- **Key insight:** Market vendors have the WORST social media because they're busy making/selling. They NEED someone else to handle it.

### Solopreneurs / Coaches / Consultants
- **Revenue:** Varies wildly ($1K-$20K/mo)
- **Marketing budget:** $200-$500/mo typical
- **What they need:** Professional presence, lead capture, booking system, social proof
- **Why $297/mo works:** One new client at $300+ pays for the month. AI chatbot + booking = they never miss a lead.
- **Key insight:** Solopreneurs don't have time to post. They need DONE-FOR-YOU, not DIY tools.

## PRICING MATH CHEAT SHEET

### How to pitch ANY niche:

1. ASK: "What's your average job/appointment/ticket value?"
2. ASK: "How many leads do you need per month to hit your goals?"
3. CALCULATE: Leads x Value x Close Rate = Monthly Revenue from Leads
4. COMPARE: Your price vs. that revenue = ROI Multiple

### Example Pitches:

**Food Truck ($297/mo):**
"5 extra customers per lunch rush at $15 each = $2,250/mo. At $297/mo, you're paying 13 cents on the dollar for marketing. That's 7.6x return. One good Saturday covers the whole month."

**Market Vendor ($297/mo):**
"You sell at 3 markets per month. If social brings 10 extra people to each market spending $25 each, that's $750 extra revenue. At $297/mo, you're more than doubling your investment."

**Plumber ($1,497/mo):**
"10 leads at $105/lead = $1,050 in lead value. At 35% close rate, 3-4 jobs = $1,200-$1,600. One job pays for the month. Everything after that is profit."

**Lawyer ($2,497/mo):**
"5 legal leads at $500/lead = $2,500 in value. One case at $2,000+ pays for the month. Your cost per acquisition drops from $1,000+ to $500."
