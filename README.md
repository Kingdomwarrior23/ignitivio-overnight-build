## KEY TRANSCRIPT INSIGHTS (Applied)

### From "5 Things Clients Actually Want" ($4M/yr agency, 350 clients):

1. **Partnership > Vendor** — Proactive updates before clients ask. Free trials for beginners.
2. **Ask What Success Means** — Same service, different metrics = one renews, one cancels.
3. **Kill Doubt in 30 Days** — Quick wins playbook: CRM setup, comment-to-DM, lead notifications.
4. **AI Demand is Real** — 300+ inbound leads/mo asking about AI. Two plays: services or white-label.
5. **Social Proof Sticks** — Clients stay when people in their real life notice their content.

### From "Pricing Calculator" (GHL agency tutorial):

- Price based on MATH not gut: niche + avg CPL + lead value + close rate = fair price
- Undercharging for high-value niches is the #1 money leak
- ROI math: if a lead is worth X and you 3x it, price is justified
- "Sticky" pricing = math makes sense so they stay longer

## MICRO-BUSINESS RESEARCH (NEW)

### Food Trucks
- Revenue: $20K-$42K/mo, margins 3-5%
- Marketing budget: $200-$500/mo (most spend less than they should)
- $297/mo = 1.4% of revenue. 5 extra customers/day = 7.6x ROI.
- Need: daily location posts, menu photos, event promotion

### Market Vendors
- Revenue: $2K-$8K/mo, budget $100-$300/mo
- Need: market day promo, new product reveals, between-market engagement
- Worst social media of any group (too busy making product)

### Solopreneurs
- Revenue: $1K-$20K/mo, budget $200-$500/mo
- Need: professional presence, lead capture, booking system
- One new client at $300+ pays for $297/mo tier
