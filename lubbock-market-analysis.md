## Owen's Workflow Breakdown (What He Didn't Show)

### Week 1: Setup for New Client

**Day 1-2: Access & Research**
- [ ] Get client's Facebook Business Manager admin access
- [ ] Add client to GHL as sub-account
- [ ] Meta Ad Library: Screenshot 20 winning ads in their industry
- [ ] Higgsfield: Generate 10 ad variations (takes 30 min total)

**Day 3: Campaign Setup**
- [ ] Create Meta campaign: "Lead Generation"
- [ ] Ad Set: $75-200/day, Advantage+ audience, age 25+
- [ ] Load all 10 ad creatives
- [ ] Create instant form with:
  - Qualifying questions (homeowner? timeline? budget?)
  - Contact info
  - GHL booking calendar embedded

**Day 4: GHL Automation**
- [ ] Connect Meta forms to GHL (Settings → Integrations → Facebook)
- [ ] Build workflow:
  ```
  New lead comes in
  → Wait 1 minute
  → SMS: "Hi [Name]! Thanks for your interest in [Service]. I'm [Client]'s AI assistant. What's the best time for a quick call?"
  → Wait for response
  → If responds: AI agent continues conversation, qualifies, books appointment
  → If no response: Follow-up SMS at 1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours
  → If qualified: Auto-book appointment on client's calendar
  → Send client notification: "New appointment booked: [Details]"
  ```

**Day 5: AI Agent Configuration**
- [ ] GHL → Conversational AI section
- [ ] Create qualification script:
  ```
  "Hi! I'm helping [Business Name] connect with qualified customers.
  
  Quick questions to make sure we're a good fit:
  1. Are you the homeowner or decision-maker?
  2. What's your timeline for this project?
  3. Do you have a budget range in mind?
  
  If yes to all: Great! Let me get you on [Owner]'s calendar. 
  Does [Day] at [Time] work, or would [Alt Day] at [Alt Time] be better?"
  ```

**Day 6-7: Test & Launch**
- [ ] Send test lead through system
- [ ] Verify SMS sends, AI responds, appointment books
- [ ] Show client their dashboard
- [ ] Turn on ads ($100/day to start)
- [ ] Monitor first 10-20 leads closely
